Y-Axis Sliding Effect

For this effect, I've used the hover pseudo-class alongside transform: translateY to create a beautiful vertical sliding motion. As the White-Cheeked Turaco in the image seems to ascend gracefully, hidden content is revealed, much like a bird rising through the lush canopy of trees. The combination of absolute positioning and smooth transitions ensures a fluid and elegant lift, perfectly reflecting the natural upward flight of birds in the forest.

X-Axis Sliding Effect

In this horizontal sliding effect, the hover pseudo-class and transform: translateX bring the image to life as the macaw glides sideways across the screen. It's like watching a bird elegantly exit between the branches! The absolute positioning and smooth transitions create a sense of movement that feels both swift and graceful, offering a delightful and engaging interaction.
